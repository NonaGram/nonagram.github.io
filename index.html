GARBTIME
Application

This app helps solve the problem of people forgetting to take out their trash by providing the schedule of garbage pickups to the community. Users can access the schedule anytime and stay informed. Download now!

ABOUT THE APP

The app notifies users of the garbage pickup schedule and provides information on proper waste segregation. It helps users never miss a pickup and dispose of their waste responsibly, reducing pollution and health risks.

FEATURES

NOTIFIES PEOPLE

The app sends timely notifications to users about the garbage pickup schedule.

INFORMATION

The app provides information to the users on proper waste segregation practices, which helps to prevent environmental pollution caused by incorrect disposal of waste.
